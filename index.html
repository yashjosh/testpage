Introduction

Search Engine Optimization (SEO) is the practice of enhancing a website to improve its visibility on search engines. This guide covers the essential aspects of SEO.

On-Page SEO

On-Page SEO involves optimizing individual web pages to rank higher and earn more relevant traffic. This includes optimizing content, HTML source code, and images.

Off-Page SEO

Off-Page SEO refers to actions taken outside of your own website to impact your rankings within search engine results pages (SERPs). This includes backlinks, social media marketing, and more.

Technical SEO

Technical SEO involves optimizing your website for the crawling and indexing phase. This includes improving site speed, mobile-friendliness, and ensuring a secure connection (HTTPS).

Conclusion

SEO is a critical component of digital marketing. By following best practices in on-page, off-page, and technical SEO, you can improve your website's visibility and drive more traffic.
